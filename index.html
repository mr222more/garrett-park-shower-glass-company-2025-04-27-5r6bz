About Garrett Park Glass

Our journey began when our founders recognized the need for more durable, aesthetically pleasing shower enclosures in the commercial sector. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty coverage for your glass installations?

We offer a comprehensive 10-year warranty covering materials and workmanship, with additional coverage for our specialized coatings and hardware components.

How long does a typical installation take?

Most commercial installations are completed within 2-3 days, depending on project scope. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your business operations.

What maintenance is required for your glass products?

Our nano-coated glass requires minimal maintenance. A simple weekly wipe-down with a soft cloth and mild cleaner is typically sufficient to maintain its pristine appearance.

Do you offer customization options?

Yes, we provide extensive customization options including glass thickness, hardware finishes, and specialized coatings to meet your specific requirements.

What safety standards do your products meet?

All our products exceed ANSI Z97.1 and CPSC 16 CFR 1201 safety standards, ensuring maximum safety for commercial applications.
